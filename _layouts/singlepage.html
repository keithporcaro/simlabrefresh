We are SIMLab.

We help people use technology to build equality.

SIMLab helps empower people to bridge the gap between information and action. We work with organizations to uncover and share honest, difficult stories and lessons from technology projects of all sizes, and develop insights and best practices from every sector that technology touches. We collect, crystallize, and publicly share these learnings, and help people apply these learnings through consultations, workshops, applied research, and prototypes. To get there, we combine ten years of institutional knowledge implementing technology projects in the worldâ€™s most difficult contexts with a team of subject matter experts and technologists.

A 501(c)(3)-exempt organization, SIMLab helps our clients better connect with the people they serve, identify accessibility gaps in systems, and maximize the potential of technology to build societies that are equitable and inclusive.
